Practice Areas

BRP Law Firm provides clients with legal advocacies and representations before the Indonesian courts. Our services include civil and bankruptcy litigations at all instances of Indonesian judiciary as well as at the Administrative and Religious Courts. We also provide legal services on criminal litigations from the stage of investigation, prosecution, and trials before the Indonesian District, High and Supreme Courts.

BRP Law Firm provides services related to the insurance legal aspects and technical insurance adjustments, insurance coverage, review and advises on claim liability. Our team is experienced with insurance coverage issues in general, including property, construction, marine hull and cargo, professional indemnity, and other insurance matters.

BRP Law Firm provides sound advice related to the implementation of production sharing contracts, transfer of rights, and liaison with government agencies in charge of mining activities. We also review technical assistance agreements in the oil, gas, and mining sectors.

BRP's partners have advised local and international clients in mergers and acquisition processes, including due diligence, data room setup, drafting and reviewing deeds of merger and acquisition, and ensuring full legal compliance.

BRP Law Firm assists local and international clients in establishing investment companies in Indonesia, guiding them through investment laws and procedures, and representing their interests before government authorities, including licensing and expatriate permits.

This service covers general labor law matters and labor disputes. BRP provides labor law advice on a retainer basis to ensure compliance with regulations and to prevent disputes before they arise.

BRP Law Firm assists boards of directors, in-house counsels, and corporate secretaries in compliance matters, contract review, and corporate governance, including commercial agreements and land acquisition issues.

BRP Law Firm provides technical assistance in contract drafting and review for local and international agreements, in both English and Bahasa Indonesia.

Based on extensive experience in development projects, BRP Law Firm provides consultancy and training for local government offices in planning, budgeting, and corruption prevention.
