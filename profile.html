Firm Profile

BRP Law Firm – Bobby Rahman & Partners is a Jakarta-based law firm providing comprehensive legal services in litigation, corporate law, investment, and international business transactions.

Our Legal Services

Our law firm provides legal services for you to meet your needs:

  • If you are a local company or individuals seeking legal advice and services in the litigation proceedings (criminal, civil including bankruptcy litigations).
  • If you are a local company or individuals having a foreign partner in conducting business in Indonesia, e.g. to draft, review, analyze or modify a contract(s).
  • If you are a foreign company or individuals willing to commence business in Indonesia either in the form of direct foreign investment company or a joint venture with a local company.
  • If your legal interests need to be represented and/or if you need legal advice and opinion in International Business Transaction, General Trading, Corporate Law and Business Law.

Our legal services are meant to ensure professional services to legally protect you in your business activities.
